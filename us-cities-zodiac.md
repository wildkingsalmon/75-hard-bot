## Notes

1. **Census-Designated Places (CDPs)**: Several entries in the population ranking are not incorporated cities but rather census-designated places or towns. These are marked with "N/A (CDP)" for incorporation date:
   - Enterprise, NV (Rank 94)
   - Spring Valley, NV (Rank 111)
   - Sunrise Manor, NV (Rank 138)
   - Paradise, NV (Rank 165)
   - Metairie, LA (Rank 221)
   - Lehigh Acres, FL (Rank 228)

2. **New York Towns**: Several New York entries (Hempstead, Brookhaven, Islip, Oyster Bay, North Hempstead, Babylon, Huntington, Ramapo, Amherst) are towns rather than cities. Their incorporation/establishment dates reflect the town's founding.

3. **Incorporation vs. Founding**: Where a city was incorporated multiple times (first as a town, then as a city), the earliest significant incorporation date is generally used. For very old cities, the charter date is preferred.

4. **Chinese Zodiac Calculation**: For cities where only the year is known (no specific month/day), the zodiac is assigned based on the calendar year. The formula used: (year - 4) mod 12, where remainder maps to: 0=Rat, 1=Ox, 2=Tiger, 3=Rabbit, 4=Dragon, 5=Snake, 6=Horse, 7=Goat, 8=Monkey, 9=Rooster, 10=Dog, 11=Pig.

5. **Consolidated City-Counties**: Augusta, GA (consolidated with Richmond County in 1995, original city incorporated 1798); Macon-Bibb County, GA (consolidated 2014, Macon incorporated 1823); Louisville, KY (consolidated with Jefferson County 2003, original city chartered 1780).

6. **Arlington, VA** (Rank 103) is a county, not a city, but is included in city population rankings as it functions as a municipality. Its organization date of 1801 is used.

7. **Washington, DC** (Rank 22) is a federal district, not an incorporated city. The date used (1790) is when Congress established it as the nation's capital.
